What Is Cryptocurrency
Before diving into signals, let us first cover the basics of crypto education for beginners. A cryptocurrency is a digital coin. It is not physical like cash, but exists on the blockchain which is like a secure online ledger.

Some famous coins are Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana. Each coin has its own purpose and value.

##How Does Crypto Trading Work
Trading means buying and selling crypto to make profit. Prices go up and down every day and traders try to take advantage of these moves.

For crypto education for beginners, it is key to know there are two ways to trade:

Short-term trading : buying and selling quickly.
Long-term holding : keeping coins for months or years.
